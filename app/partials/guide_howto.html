How to Copy Your Guide into a Word Processor

Read through the instructions first.

  1. You probably want to open the word processor and start a new document first, though you could do it after the copy.
  2. Click the "Show Guide" link below.
  3. A new tab will open with your document.
  4. Select the entire document. This most easily done by using Ctrl-A.
  5. Copy, most easily done by using Ctrl-C.
  6. Now the document is in your clipboard.
  7. Go to your word processing document and paste, most easily done using Ctrl-V.
  8. Close the tab.
