This app will enable you to build your own customized interview guide. An interview has the following structure:

  • The top level is the domain. There are currently {{domainsLength}} domains. To learn more click the Domains link on the left.
  • Each domain is divided into constructs.
  • Each construct contains pertinent questions.
  • A question may or may not have probes.
Theory of Operation
  • Choose a domain
  • Choose the constructs you feel are pertinent
  • For each construct choose the questions you want included in your guide
  • Repeat until guide is complete
When you are done you can download your guide as an editable Word document.
