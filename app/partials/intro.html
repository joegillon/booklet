The Interview Guide Tool will help build a customized interview guide based on the CFIR constructs that are the focus of an evaluation. Click here to view the CFIR taxonomy:

How to Navigate Site (see links above)

  • Choose Interview Questions: Choose this option to select domains, constructs, questions for your customized interview guide.
  • Get Guide: Choose this option after you've selected all the questions you want. Then follow the instructions for copying your guide to a word processor.
  • Start Over: Your question choices are cumulative. You can choose some questions, get a guide, then choose more questions. The ensuing guide will contain ALL the questions you've chosen during the session - unless you click "Start Over".
  • Main Site: Choose this option to return to the main CFIR site.

Advice
  1. These questions are offered as a starting point â€“ there is great latitude in how questions can be worded; concepts within constructs may not be covered by the available set of questions.
  2. Questions will most likely need to be adapted to your evaluation.
  3. Questions can (and most likely should) be reordered to create an effective interview structure.
  4. In most scenarios, interviews should be semi-structured: questions may be asked in varying order based on the participant and not all questions may be asked of all participants depending on their role and other considerations.
  5. Consider prefacing these questions with broad open-ended questions to help establish rapport and to elicit grounding stories in the words of interviewees. These questions will vary depending on your scenario.
